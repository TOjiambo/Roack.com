Property Description

Welcome to this extraordinary Mediterranean-inspired villa estate, where timeless elegance meets contemporary luxury. Perched on a pristine hillside with panoramic ocean views, this architectural masterpiece represents the pinnacle of coastal living.

The residence spans over 6,500 square feet of meticulously crafted living space, featuring soaring ceilings, floor-to-ceiling windows, and seamless indoor-outdoor flow. Every detail has been thoughtfully designed to create an atmosphere of refined sophistication and understated grandeur.

The gourmet chef's kitchen is a culinary enthusiast's dream, equipped with top-of-the-line appliances, custom Italian cabinetry, and a magnificent center island with exotic marble countertops. Adjacent to the kitchen, the formal dining room offers spectacular sunset views through expansive glass doors that open to the infinity pool terrace.

The master suite is a private sanctuary occupying the entire upper level, complete with a spa-inspired bathroom, dual walk-in closets, and a private balcony overlooking the endless ocean horizon. Four additional en-suite bedrooms provide luxurious accommodations for family and guests.

The outdoor living spaces are equally impressive, featuring a resort-style infinity pool, multiple entertainment terraces, outdoor kitchen with pizza oven, and beautifully landscaped gardens with Mediterranean plantings. This is not just a home—it's a lifestyle destination.
